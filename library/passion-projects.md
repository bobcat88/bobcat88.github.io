# Passion Projects

These projects are not positioned as client missions. They are evidence of curiosity, autonomy and strategic direction: what Johan builds when no one is looking.

## Positioning Role

Use these projects to support roles involving:
- Data & IA strategy
- AI governance
- knowledge management
- PMO automation
- decision support
- LLM orchestration

Do not overstate them as enterprise deployments. Frame them as personal systems, active experiments and working prototypes that sharpen Johan's professional practice.
